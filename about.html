README

Hello, I'm Spencer!

I am passionate about crafting thoughtful, intentional, and delightful user experiences. I have been developing for Apple platforms since the introduction of the Swift Programming Language in 2014. After a few years of experimenting with Swift and UIKit, I developed an app for my high school to help drive engagement with local volunteer opportunities.

In 2019, I joined Apple while also beginning studies at UC San Diego. At Apple, I've had the opportunity to contribute my Swift / SwiftUI skills to the Home app. I hope to cotinue my work at Apple and contribute even more, especially in areas outside of my comfort zone of iOS.

I love to learn new skills, so I use my personal time to explore these areas of interest through projects that combine hardware, software, and design. You can learn more about my projects here.
