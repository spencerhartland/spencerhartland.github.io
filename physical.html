Privacy

Updated: March 26, 2026

Physical ("the App") is a digital catalogue of your music on physical media. The App runs entirely on your device.


Data Collection The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no third-party services. All data you enter remains on your device and under your control.


Children's Privacy Because the App collects no data whatsoever, there are no special concerns regarding children's use of the App.
